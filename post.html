Atilano's is a resturaunt chain that prides itself on being open 24/7. The chain has been arounf for a long time, and as of 2022 have been expanding all the way from close by Gonzaga campus to all the way out in the valley.

Cochinito Taqueria

Opening in 2020 just when the pandemic was losening up, Cochinito opened their doors to a hungry Spokane city. This place serves higher-brow and more up-scale Mexican food. This restaurant strives to be a very nice sit down place in the heart of downtown Spokane.

Some quotes from customers:
"They have the best Birria tacos in the PNW!" - Lily R

"The atmosphere is great, but the food is a tad bit overpriced" Austin F

Taco Time!

Taco time presents itself as the best fast food Mexican option in the greater Spokane area. Taco Time is an established chain across the PNW that has been serving customers since 1959.

Some quotes from the customers:
